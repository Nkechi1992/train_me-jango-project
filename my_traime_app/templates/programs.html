3-Weeks Coding Camp for Beginners

Learners will gain basic knowledge of the most famous industry based front-end languages (HTML, CSS & Javascript). At the end of the programme, participants will be able to develop their first project with HTML, CSS and Javascript. This programme aims at young women who have interest in developing a career in the technology sector.

  • Duration: 3-Weeks
  • Cost: Free
  • Eligibility: Must be a woman between 16-35

  • Cost: Free
  • Eligibility: Must be a woman between 16-35
